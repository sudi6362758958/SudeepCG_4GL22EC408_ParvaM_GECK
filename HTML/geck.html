Government Engineering College, Kushalanagar was established in the year 2007. It is located in Karnataka, and is established as a public institute. The institute owns several approved and recognised courses from nationally renowned institutions such as AICTE. The institute offers UG courses in Full Time. It offers 4 courses in total including specialisations in Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering. Furthermore, the institute offers courses such as B.E. / B.Tech. Government Engineering College, Kushalanagar offers these courses in Engineering stream. Furthermore, the Government Engineering College, Kushalanagar offers an annual intake of 240 students. Campus Location Karnataka Courses Offered UG No. of Seat 240.

Admisson Details

  • Eligibility:Candidates must have passed the 12th standard with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with Chemistry/Bio-Technology/Biology/Electronics/Computer Science as optional subjects. A minimum of 45% aggregate marks is required.
  • Admission Process: Admissions are conducted through the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET)
  • Fee Structure: The annual tuition fee for the B.E. program is INR 38,000 for Karnataka students and INR 90,000 for non-Karnataka students.
